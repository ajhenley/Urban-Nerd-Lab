KillChain

The Cybersecurity Card Game

FEATURES:
  • Models the cyber landscape and the factors that students should be aware of.
  • Allows students to combine a variety of attacks, exploit/payload combinations, command line tools and mitigations to simulate fighting a cyberwar.
  • Students are able to mimic strategies used in real attacks.
  • Students are able to come up with their own strategies.
  • Most Importantly: Shows the value of defense in depth.

Packets

The Shattering

FEATURES:
  • Using playing cards demonstrates transmission of both files and streams over packet switching networks.
  • Students emulate the behavior of routers, switches and hubs.
  • Demonstrates the transmission of TCP and UDP.
  • Demonstrates BGP, ICMP, DoS, and DDoS.

Bunny Web

The Vulnerable Web Application

FEATURES:
  • Presents easy to use example applications for examining the most common OWASP 10 vulnerabilities.
  • A single cohesive application that has all the vulnerabilities, but isn’t just a stub application.
  • Scannable by ZAP and Burp Suite.
  • Useful as test app for sqlmap.
